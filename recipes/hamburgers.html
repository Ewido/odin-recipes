Hamburgers

Hamburgers...Juicy and Delicious!!

This recipe is sure to delight and leave you feeling satisfied.

Just follow the recipe, insert some love and watch as your diners moan with utter satisfaction!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Hamburger Buns
  • Seasoning
  • Ketchup, Mustard, Mayo
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Pickles
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Directions

  1. Mix ground beef and seasoning then form into patties.
  2. Cook patties approximately 4 minutes each side.
  3. Prepare condiments (lettuce, Tomato, Onion)
  4. Assemble patties and condiments.
  5. top with Pickles
