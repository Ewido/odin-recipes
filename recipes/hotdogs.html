Hot Dogs


Always a staple, Hot Dogs have stood the test of time.


This dish, while simple to prepare, can be as simple or complex to the palette as you desire.


Ingredients:

  • Hot Dogs
  • Buns
  • Ketchup & Mustard
  • Toppings of Choice
    • Relish
    • Onions
    • Cheese
    • Etc...

Directions:

  1. Use your preferred method to cook your Hot Dogs:
    • Tip: Hot Dogs typically have better flavor when cooked on the grill.
  2. Place cooked dogs in buns.
  3. Top dogs with your desired toppings and enjoy!
