Spaghetti


Pasta! We all love pasta!


Follow this recipe for a delicious pasta meal the family is sure to enjoy?


Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Spaghetti Sauce
  • Ground Hamburger
  • Garlic Powder
  • Garlic Bread

Directions:

  1. Bring 4 quarts of water to a boil.
  2. Add spaghetti noodles to water and boil as package directs.
  3. In skillet, brown hamburger then drain excess grease.
  4. Return cooked hamburger to skillet and add spaghetti sauce.
  5. Add Garlic Powder to sauce and stir in.
  6. Drain spaghetti noodles.
  7. Cook garlic bread.
